Jeevan Lakshya

A Non-Linked, Participating, Limited Premium Payment, Life Insurance Plan

Plan Overview

Jeevan Lakshya is a unique protection and savings insurance plan that ensures your child's future even in your absence. It provides financial protection to the family and guarantees the child's education and other needs.

Education Security

Guaranteed education funding for your child

Family Protection

Regular income benefit for family in case of unfortunate event

Savings Benefit

Maturity benefit with bonus additions

Policy Benefits Timeline

During Policy Term

Regular premium payment and life cover continues

In Case of Death

Family receives immediate lump sum and regular monthly income

Education Milestones

Guaranteed payouts at key education stages

At Maturity

Maturity sum assured plus bonuses paid

Key Benefits

  • Guaranteed monthly income to family in case of death
  • Premium waiver benefit on death of policyholder
  • Lump sum payment at maturity with bonuses
  • Additional protection through optional riders
  • Limited premium payment term options
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C & 10(10D)
