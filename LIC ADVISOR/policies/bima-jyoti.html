Bima Jyoti

A Non-Linked, Non-Participating, Individual, Savings, Life Insurance Plan

Plan Overview

Bima Jyoti is a guaranteed return plan that offers both insurance protection and savings. It provides guaranteed additions and a lump sum amount at maturity, making it an ideal choice for long-term financial planning.

Guaranteed Returns

Fixed guaranteed additions throughout the policy term

Life Cover

Financial protection for your family

Flexible Options

Choice of policy term and premium payment modes

Guaranteed Benefits

Death Benefit

Higher of:

  • Sum Assured on Death
  • 105% of total premiums paid
  • Guaranteed Sum Assured on Maturity
Maturity Benefit

Sum of:

  • Basic Sum Assured
  • Guaranteed Additions
  • Final Additional Guarantee

Key Features

  • Guaranteed Additions of ₹50 per thousand Sum Assured per year
  • Final Additional Guarantee at maturity
  • Option to receive death benefit in installments
  • Loan facility available after 2 years
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C & 10(10D)
  • Multiple premium payment modes available
