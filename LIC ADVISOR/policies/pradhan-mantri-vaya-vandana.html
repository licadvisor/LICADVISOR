Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana

A Government of India Backed Pension Scheme for Senior Citizens

Government Backed Scheme

Secure your retirement with a pension scheme backed by the Government of India, offering a guaranteed rate of 7.40% p.a. for the entire policy term.

Scheme Overview

PMVVY is a pension scheme exclusively for senior citizens aged 60 years and above, offering guaranteed returns for 10 years.

Guaranteed Returns

Fixed pension rate of 7.40% p.a.

Regular Income

Monthly/Quarterly/Half-yearly/Yearly

High Investment Limit

Up to ₹15 Lakhs per senior citizen
