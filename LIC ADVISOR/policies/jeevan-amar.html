Jeevan Amar

A Non-Linked, Non-Participating, Individual, Pure Risk Premium, Life Insurance Plan

Plan Overview

Jeevan Amar is a comprehensive term insurance plan that provides financial protection to your family in case of unfortunate events. It offers high coverage at affordable premiums with flexible premium payment options.

High Coverage

Get coverage up to ₹1 Crore at affordable premiums

Flexible Terms

Choose policy terms from 10 to 40 years

Tax Benefits

Get tax benefits under Section 80C & 10(10D)

Key Benefits

  • Life cover up to age 80 years
  • Option to choose from Regular Premium or Single Premium payment
  • Additional coverage through optional riders
  • Special premium rates for female lives
  • Option to receive death benefit in lump sum or monthly installments
  • Premium waiver for specially-abled dependent lives

Eligibility Criteria
