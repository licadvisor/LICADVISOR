Jeevan Anand

A Unique Combination of Whole Life and Endowment Plan

Plan Overview

Jeevan Anand is a unique plan that provides life cover even after maturity. It combines the features of an endowment plan and a whole life plan, offering dual benefits to the policyholder.

Lifetime Coverage

Protection continues after maturity

Maturity Benefit

Lump sum payment at maturity

Bonus Benefits

Participate in profits

Unique Features

During Policy Term
  • Regular premium payments
  • Life cover equal to Sum Assured
  • Bonus accumulation
At Maturity
  • Maturity Sum Assured
  • Vested Bonuses
  • Final Additional Bonus (if any)
After Maturity
  • Continued life cover
  • Equal to basic Sum Assured
  • No further premium payment

Key Benefits

Death Benefit

Before Maturity:

  • Sum Assured + Vested Bonuses + Final Additional Bonus (if any)

After Maturity:

  • Basic Sum Assured without bonuses
Maturity Benefit
  • Basic Sum Assured
  • Vested Simple Reversionary Bonuses
  • Final Additional Bonus (if declared)
  • Continued life cover equal to basic Sum Assured
